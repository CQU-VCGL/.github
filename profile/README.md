# Hi there 👋 Welcome to VCGL

## Visual Computing and Geometric Learning (VCGL)

We are the **Visual Computing and Geometric Learning (VCGL)** Lab, conducting research at the intersection of:

- 🎨 Computer Graphics  
- 👁️ Computer Vision  
- 🤖 Embodied AI  
- 🌍 3D Vision and Scene Understanding  
- ✨ Neural Rendering / 3D Gaussian Splatting / Generative Modeling  

Our mission is to advance the frontier of **graphics and visual intelligence**, spanning from foundational algorithms to real-world embodied applications.

---

## 🔬 Research Highlights

Our recent research focuses include:

- Real-time Neural Rendering and 3D Gaussian Splatting  
- Dynamic Scene Reconstruction and Editing  
- Neural Surface and Geometry Processing  
- Physically-grounded Digital Humans and Avatars  
- Embodied Perception and Robotics Data Generation  
- Generative AI for 3D Content Creation

## 🌈 Open Source Philosophy

We believe research should be:

- Reproducible  
- Open and collaborative  
- Impactful beyond papers  

We actively release:

- Code  
- Benchmarks  
- Datasets  
- Research tools  

Contributions, issues and collaborations are welcome.

Many repositories in this organization are maintained as forks corresponding to codebases from our lab's papers and research projects.

If you find them useful, please consider visiting and giving a ⭐ to the original repositories.

We appreciate the open-source community and all original contributors.
